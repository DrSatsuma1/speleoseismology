# Genoese Archival Research for 1285 Dark Earthquake Validation

## Goal
Find statistical anomaly in Genoese notarial records mid-1285 (document production gap) followed by surge in death-bed wills/testaments in 1286. This would provide independent human-record validation of the geochemically-detected earthquake.

## Key Archive
**Archivio di Stato di Genova (ASGe)** - Notai Antichi collection
- World's oldest notarial archive (from 1154 CE)
- **150 cartularies from 13th century** (more than all of Europe combined)
- 450 units from 14th century

## What We're Looking For

**Ideal finding**: Time-series of documents per month, 1280-1290, showing:
```
1284: ~12/month baseline
1285: GAP (June-September near zero)
1286: BOUNCE-BACK (25-30/month surge in wills/testaments)
```

**Alternative indicators**:
- Spike in death-bed gifts (legati pro anima)
- Surge in property transfers (inheritances)
- Orphan guardianship appointments
- Well-deepening/repair contracts (hydrological disruption)
