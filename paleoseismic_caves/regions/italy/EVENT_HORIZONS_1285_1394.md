# Event Horizons: 1285 & 1394 Dark Earthquakes in Basura Core (BA18-4)

## Purpose

This document provides exact sample positions for the two "Dark Earthquakes" detected in the Basura Cave speleothem. These positions are critical for:
1. **Physical sampling** - targeting U-Th dating of event layers
2. **Speleoseismite surveys** - locating broken formations at corresponding depths
3. **Publication figures** - precise axis positioning for time series plots

## Speleoseismite Search Protocol

If broken speleothems exist from these events, they should be found at or near:

### 1285 Event
- **Target depth**: 32-33 mm from apex in BA18-4
- **Corresponding cave stratigraphy**: Look for broken stalactites/stalagmites with U-Th ages in range 1237-1322 CE
- **Expected features**: Broken tips, tilted formations, calcite "bridges" across fractures

### 1394 Event
- **Target depth**: 27-28 mm from apex in BA18-4
- **Corresponding cave stratigraphy**: U-Th ages in range 1377-1403 CE
- **Expected features**: Smaller breaks (shallower rupture), possible regrowth over fractures
