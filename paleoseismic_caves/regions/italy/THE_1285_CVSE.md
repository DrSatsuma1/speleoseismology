## 2.3 Statistical Significance Test

**Hypothesis**:
- H₀: The 1285 anomaly is a regional climatic event affecting all caves
- H₁: The 1285 anomaly is isolated to the Alpine-Ligurian seismic corridor

- P(|z| > 2) = 0.046 (probability of extreme value at any cave)
- P(|z| < 1.5) = 0.87 (probability of normal value)

- 2 caves show |z| > 2 (Bàsura, Klapferloch)
- 4 caves show |z| < 1.5 (Bunker, Villars, Jeita, Ifoulki)

```
P = 0.046 × (0.87)^4 = 0.026
```

**Result**: p = 0.026 (significant at α = 0.05)
