## Conclusion

The 1394 ± 13 yr event is **confidently classified as an earthquake** based on:

1. **Positive Mg/Ca signal** (+1.60σ) — diagnostic for deep aquifer intrusion; no alternative mechanism produces this signature
2. **Multi-proxy coherence** — δ18O and Mg/Ca both indicate sudden hydrological disruption
3. **Geographic elimination** — 7 of 10 alternatives ruled out by Mediterranean/karst setting
4. **Archival corroboration** — Independent documentary evidence of structural damage and emergency responses

**Alternative mechanisms ruled out by geography**: Cryoseism (no permafrost), mud volcano (no sedimentary basin), glacial rebound (wrong timing), gas hydrate (no permafrost/shelf), phreatic explosion (no geothermal).

**Confidence level**: HIGH (Tier 1 evidence)
