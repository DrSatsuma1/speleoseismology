## Key Findings

1. **71 dark earthquake candidates** across 15 regions on 6 continents

2. **8 blind-validated events** confirm methodology works

3. **Recovery time** is the most robust seismic discriminant:
   - >50 years = definitive seismic
   - 10-50 years = probable seismic
   - <10 years = ambiguous

4. **Bunker Cave anomaly cluster** (5 events, 97-841 CE) suggests unrecognized Lower Rhine Graben seismicity

5. **CVSE pattern** (compound volcanic-seismic events) identified at 4 sites:
   - Italy 1285 CE
   - Belize 936 CE, 1159 CE
   - Pacific NW 853 CE

6. **Oldest events**:
   - Cuba ~8,756 BP (prehistoric)
   - Oregon T11 ~3940 BCE (Cascadia)
   - Brazil ~96 CE (intraplate)
