### Methodology Summary

**Speleothems**: δ18O anomalies reflect earthquake-induced groundwater chemistry changes via the [Chiodini model](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/abs/pii/S0012821X11000872) (CO2 flux → aquifer perturbation → drip water isotope shift).

**Tree Rings**: DIVERGENCE between adjacent trees (some suppressed, some enhanced) indicates seismic damage rather than climate. Validated against 1906 M7.9 - all trees enhanced (no local damage), vs 1580 where some trees crashed to z=-3.25 (direct fault damage).
