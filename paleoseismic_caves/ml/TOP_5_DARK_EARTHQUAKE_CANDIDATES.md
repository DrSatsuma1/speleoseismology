## Methodology Notes

### Recovery Time as Primary Discriminant

This triage confirms that **recovery time** is the most robust seismic discriminant:

| Mechanism | Recovery Time | Examples |
|-----------|---------------|----------|
| Volcanic | 1-7 years | 1257 Samalas (3 yr), 1284 UE6 (2 yr) |
| Climatic (drought) | 1-5 years | Decadal oscillations |
| **Seismic** | **10-212 years** | Shenqi 212 yr, Lapa Grande 71 yr |

The order-of-magnitude gap between seismic (10-212 yr) and volcanic/climatic (1-7 yr) recovery makes this discriminant nearly unambiguous for events with >20-year recovery.

### Z-Score Threshold Re-evaluation

Bunker Cave (z=-1.73σ, 57 yr recovery) demonstrates that strict z-score thresholds may miss valid seismic events. **Recovery time should override z-score when >30 years**.
